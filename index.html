SaardhaOn-demand local delivery

One platform. Four interfaces.

Saardha connects local street vendors and small shops with salaried, location-tracked Saradhis for on-demand delivery. Each interface shares a single live datastore — an order placed in the Customer app appears instantly in the Merchant, Admin, and Saradhi views.
